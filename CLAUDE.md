## Project

MyTurn is a web-based children's English speaking classroom MVP focused on recreating the live feel of a real online small-group class. The current milestone is a single 15-minute lesson with one teacher, one real child, and one AI classmate, optimized for landscape tablet and driven by image-based oral practice.
